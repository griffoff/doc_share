4. VR Meetings with Microsoft Mesh

This initiative investigates the viability of using Virtual Reality (VR) technology, specifically Microsoft Mesh, to conduct internal meetings. The goal is to determine if VR can serve as a cost-effective and engaging alternative to some in-person travel and a more immersive option than standard video conferences.

Status: Defining Success Criteria for Trial

Goal: Evaluate if VR can reduce travel costs and offer a superior alternative to traditional video calls for certain meeting types.

Success Criteria (Qualitative):
  • Conduct trials for various scenarios (brainstorming, readouts, standups).
  • Gather user feedback to determine if the experience is better than video calls.
  • Assess if VR would be an acceptable replacement for a significant portion (e.g., 1 in 4) of physical meetings.

Next Step: Awaiting Karan's travel cost analysis to establish a financial baseline for the trial.
