3. ETL Modernization: Reducing Infrastructure Spend

This is a strategic cost-saving initiative to replace our high-cost Informatica license. The goal is a significant, direct reduction in our data infrastructure budget while improving the scalability of our data pipelines.

SMART Goal: Complete the evaluation of Astronomer as a viable replacement for Informatica by **EOY 2025**, positioning us to realize significant licensing cost savings before the next renewal cycle.

Objective: Vet "Astronomer" (Apache Airflow) as a viable, lower-cost alternative to Informatica.

Progress: Engaged the primary stakeholder group to ensure their requirements are met, de-risking the migration and securing buy-in.

Next Step: Define an experimental phase with Wes and Rajesh to produce a data-driven recommendation.

4. VR Meetings: Optimizing Travel & Collaboration Budgets

This R&D initiative will determine if VR can produce a tangible ROI by reducing corporate travel expenses. The goal is to validate if VR is a cost-effective substitute for a percentage of physical meetings.

SMART Goal: By **EOY 2025**, complete a proof-of-concept to validate if VR can achieve a **25% reduction ($125,000+) in the annual internal travel budget** by replacing in-person meetings.

Success Criteria: Confirm through user trials that VR is a superior collaborative experience to video calls and an acceptable substitute for a target number of physical meetings.

Next Step: Finalize the business case by benchmarking potential travel savings against implementation costs, pending Karan's analysis.

5. AI Support: Maximizing Executive Impact

This initiative aims to maximize the return on our CEO's time at key events. By using AI to automate and deepen the briefing process, we can ensure he is positioned for the most strategic conversations, directly benefiting partnerships and corporate strategy.

SMART Goal: Implement a functional AI-powered briefing process for all executive events by **Q1 2026**, aiming to reduce preparation time by at least 25% and demonstrably improve post-event strategic outcomes.

Proposed Solution: Utilize Gemini and NotebookLM to create hyper-relevant, context-aware briefings, turning preparation time into a strategic advantage.

ROI: The return is measured in the quality of outcomes from executive engagements, such as new partnerships or strategic opportunities.

SMART Goal: Implement an interim AI process to reduce initial playbook draft time by **10% by EOY 2025**, capturing immediate efficiency gains while awaiting a long-term platform decision.

7. AI Presentations: Radically Reducing Content Costs

This R&D effort has proven we can fundamentally reduce the cost and time of content creation. The business goal is to unlock these massive efficiency gains across the company by finding a secure, scalable sharing solution.

SMART Goal: Unlock **$1,000,000 in annual productivity savings** by deploying an AI presentation workflow that reduces creation time by 50% (saving ~10 FTEs). The next step is to identify a secure hosting solution by **Q1 2026**.

Key Achievement & ROI: Demonstrated a reduction in creation time from weeks to hours. A pessimistic estimate of halving the time (2 days to 1) across ~2,500 annual presentations saves 2,500 days of work.

Blocker to ROI: The inability to share these assets securely in our corporate environment (e.g., SharePoint) prevents us from capturing this value at scale.

Next Step: Prioritize investigation of a secure hosting solution to unlock this efficiency gain for the entire organization.
