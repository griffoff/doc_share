A New Partnership for Knowledge

We're not just adding another tool. We're creating a powerful ecosystem with two core partners, each with a specific job to do.

The Discovery Engine: Atolio

This is your **intelligent starting point**. Atolio connects to all our systems, using AI to find the most relevant information, no matter where it's hidden. It's the powerful search that understands our chaos.

The Curated Library: Confluence

This is our **single source of truth**. Confluence is the permanent, organized home for our most valuable knowledge—project plans, best practices, official documentation, and meeting notes.

How It Works: From Discovery to Curation

This isn't a massive cleanup project. It's a simple, continuous cycle of improvement that everyone can participate in, driven by our real-world needs.

1. Ask Atolio

Start every search in Atolio. Ask a natural question, just like you would in Google.

2. Find the Answer

Atolio searches everywhere and finds the answer, even if it's buried in an old Teams chat or a forgotten SharePoint site. You get your answer fast.

3. Recognize Value

You realize this information is valuable and shouldn't be so hard to find again. This is the "curation trigger."

4. Curate in Confluence

Take 5 minutes to capture that knowledge. Create a clean, simple page in our Confluence library with a clear title. You've just turned tribal knowledge into a permanent asset.

5. The System Improves

The next time someone searches, Atolio's AI will recognize the new, authoritative Confluence page and rank it as the top result. The entire system gets smarter because of you.
